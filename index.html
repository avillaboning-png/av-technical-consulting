Methodology: From Diagnosis to Control

1
Technical Diagnosis

Collection of information on processes, substances, tasks and existing controls. Visual inspections, document review and structured interviews to understand how work is really performed.

2
Risk Analysis

Identification of chemical, mechanical and physical hazards, evaluation of exposure scenarios and definition of critical points. The analysis links occupational risk with regulatory and operational impacts.

3
Controls and Implementation Roadmap

Design of practical control measures, procedures, checklists and training content. Prioritized action plans enable companies to advance in stages while reducing risk in a measurable way.
