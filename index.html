Professional Profile

SST & Engineering Consultant

Focused on preventing occupational accidents and diseases through chemical, mechanical and environmental risk control. The practice integrates industrial engineering, safety engineering and real field experience across multiple sectors.

AV Technical Consulting supports organizations through technical assessments, remote and on-site audits, risk mapping, procedure design and targeted training programs. The objective is to translate regulations and standards into practical, engineering-based controls that work in real operations and protect workers.

Core Competence in Occupational Risk

  • Chemical exposure and compatibility risk in industrial, educational and laboratory environments.
  • Mechanical hazard control in equipment, tools and maintenance tasks.
  • Physical and environmental conditions affecting worker health and process reliability.
  • Alignment with SG-SST requirements, ARL programs and regulatory frameworks.

Core Consulting Services

Methodology: From Diagnosis to Control

1 ¬∑ Technical Diagnosis

Collection of information on processes, substances, tasks and existing controls. Visual inspections, document review and structured interviews to understand how work is actually performed on site.

2 ¬∑ Risk Analysis

Identification of chemical, mechanical and physical hazards, evaluation of exposure scenarios and definition of critical points. The analysis links occupational risk with regulatory, health and operational impacts.

3 ¬∑ Controls & Implementation Roadmap

Design of practical control measures, procedures, checklists and training content. Prioritized action plans enable companies to advance in stages while reducing risk in a measurable and auditable way.
